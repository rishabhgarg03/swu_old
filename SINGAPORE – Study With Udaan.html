SINGAPORE

WHY STUDY IN SINGAPORE

Singapore is among the most visited and respected countries in Asia. It is a lively city-state with diverse culture and a welcoming nature. With a literacy rate of 96%, Singapore has maintained a great reputation for science and research. The city-state has high standards for education and lures in international students in a massive number. Indian students are opting for Singapore as their preferred study destination as the country offers degrees of such a high value that both Indian and MNCs recruit such students. Singapore has a lot of courses and programmes to offer. It offers Universities, institutes of technical education, polytechnics, and several other educational institutions. Degree courses along with diploma and special courses are also available.

1. Education system is one of the best

Various global reports recognize Singaporean institutions as seats of world-leading research and innovation. The 2015 INSEAD Global Innovation Index ranked it seventh, IMD’s 2014 World Competitiveness Rankings ranked it third and the World Economic Forum’s Global Competitiveness Report for 2014-15 ranked it second in this regard. Students planning to pursue their degree from universities of Singapore can choose from a wide range of subject areas such as law, computer science, mechanical engineering, materials science, statistics, accounting and finance, communication and media studies, and pharmacy.

2. Top-quality institutions

There are 34 universities in Singapore. Some public universities are also open to both Singaporean and foreign students. The National University of Singapore (NUS) and Nanyang Technological University (NTU), the two well-known universities are also ranked in the world’s top 15. Singapore is the first Asian nation to enter the top 10 of the Universitas 21 rankings, which evaluates academic institutions on the basis of their investment, gender balance, international connectivity, and research output.

3. Home to many foreign universities

Thanks to Singapore’s links with other foreign universities, there are several regional campuses. Although these universities are private and have a smaller number of students, they offer both undergraduate and postgraduate degrees. Partnered courses are offered by local institutions, which is a result of good ties with American and Australian institutions. International universities like James Cook University Singapore, University of Nevada, Curtin Singapore and Amity University are a few.

4. A global business hub

Singapore is known for its global links and its flourishing business. It offers the population to witness great changes and never fails to bring pride to the hard work of the people. Singapore is a global business hub, and has an open business policy, an advanced infrastructure and political stability.

5. Ample scholarships, funds and bursaries opportunities

A great setback of foreign universities is the level of stress it brings along with it. Although a homeland education might seem more comfortable, the University of Singapore offers ample of opportunities in terms of funds for students. Grants are available for international students. Singapore’s Students gets subsidies if enrolled in full-time diploma or undergraduate programme in some of the selected universities. This is offered by The Tuition Grant Scheme (TGS), started by the country’s Ministry of Education.In return, the students must stay back and work in the country for at least 3 years.

6. Cost-effective

Singapore invests highly in its reputation and maintenance. It gets the upper hand as it challenges the quality of education of international universities and the tuition fee and living expenses are a lot less than that of the U.S.A. and the U.K. Abundant facilities to make life easier and comfortable are provided. Cheap and efficient services like public transportation, are also offered.

7. A great place to live in

Singapore is as mesmerizing as a place could be. A linguistic hub with people talking in more than 4 different languages. You get to learn and experience a lot from just conversing with the people. The city streets are happening and equally safe, as the government imparts strict laws. Travel websites have talked highly of Singapore and has also ranks its as one the most safest cities in the world. Singapore is also strategically located in the heart of Asia and can be a hub to explore the South-east Asian region. It is well-connected to the rest of Asia as Singapore has one of the best airports in the region.

TOP COURSES

Universities in Singapore offer every student a variety of courses and programmes as per their area of interest. Whether it’s an undergraduate or postgraduate programme a student wants to enrol in, there are endless options on one’s platter. Universities offer students a diverse spectrum of courses that have multidisciplinary and cross-faculty approaches. Singapore is popular among students who wish to study design, music, animation, and game development, etc. Courses in law and combined post-graduate courses in management and engineering are also extremely popular choices for students from South-Asia. In addition, business education at Singapore institutions is considered top-notch owing to the country being a global business leader.

Top courses to study in Singapore:
